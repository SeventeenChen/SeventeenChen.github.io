Samples

Samples description:

Clean: the clean speech

Narrow Band: the narrow band speech which is in the range of 300–3400 Hz and sampled at the rate of 8,000 samples per second according to the codec of G.711

PE Loss: the speech generated from NN with entropy loss

PM Loss: the speech generated from NN with mpeg loss

MSE Loss: the speech generated from NN with mean square root loss

PWE Loss: the speech generated from NN with perceptual loss
